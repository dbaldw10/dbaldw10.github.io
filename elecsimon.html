the artist formerly known as

Elec Simon

Elec Simon has always taken care of his friends, family, and kids. Whether its in the Cleveland Cav's Arena during halftime, in the school gymnasium giving a vunerable true life story, or in the parking lot helping an old woman with her groceries, he has always done what he can for every person around him.

A humble country boy ohio start led Elec Simon to the big NY at a young age, and landed him a spotlight on the biggest Off Broadway Show in History, "Stomp". There he was apart of the cast for a decade, and currently takes parts in shows when needed. The Stomp family is always a family.

The excitement you see on stage, the stories you hear him tell, and the tattoos on his skin, all give you a clue into who Elec is. But nothing will let you get to know him, like when you hear him laugh. Its unmistakeable, and bring a light to any person that hears it. And dont forget "If you fall down, get your mother fuckin ass up bitch."
